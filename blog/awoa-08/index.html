A Week of A-Frame 8

Mar 11, 2016

What's up with A-Frame for the week of Mar 4, 2016 to Mar 11, 2016.

A-Frame is a web framework for building VR experiences.

Projects

  • withersc built Kinect Scans @ Makeathon —

    Students 3D-scanned with Xbox Kinect sensors and exported to .OBJ models. The winning project at University of Michigan’s Makeathon 2016.

  • drryanjames built CadaVR —

    Medical education with a virtual cadaver, leveraging structural models of anatomy, natural input, and mathematical models of physiology.

  • scenevr built Subreddit Gallery —

    View live subreddit images in a virtual gallery.

  • swamphousegroup built First-Person Sh*tter —

    A rough night leads to an unmaintained restroom.

  • zenspotio built Zenspot —

    Relaxing nature experiences with soothing music.

  • 32studios built 32Studios Lounge —

    Enjoy a lounge with coffee machines, laptops, and VR headsets while grooving to SoundCloud.

  • washingtonpost built Mars: An Interactive Journey —

    In collaboration with a group at the University of Texas at Austin, The Washington Post published a feature on the Red Planet and space exploration programs.
