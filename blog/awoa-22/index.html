A Week of A-Frame 22

Jul 22, 2016

What's up with A-Frame for the week of Jul 15, 2016 to Jul 22, 2016.

A-Frame is a web framework for creating virtual reality experiences with HTML.

Tracked controllers has landed! Use the HTC Vive controllers with A-Frame. NPR has published a WebVR site, rondagdag hooked up A-Frame to control a robot with Leap Motion, and we now have an initial community code of conduct!

0.3.0 is still in progress, and several members of the team will be out next week. We need to land improved embedded scene support, fix the resolution quality regression on VR mobile, implement built-in link traversal support, and refresh the homepage. We may publish an official beta build next week to hold over depending on our progress.
