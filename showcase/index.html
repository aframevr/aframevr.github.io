A-Frame

Showcase

A-Frame has been used in production by companies such as Al Jazeera, Amnesty International, CERN, Chevrolet, Google, Samsung, Disney, Ford, Magic Leap, Mozilla, NASA, NPR, Sony, Supermedium, and the Washington Post!

This showcase links to a range of experiences that are available online today, that show the range of what can be built using A-Frame.

  • Moon Rider by Supermedium

    Surf the musical road among the moon, stars, and lights.

  • Soundboxing WebVR by Eric Florenzano (with Supermedium)

    A VR music kickboxing game!

  • Towermax Fitness by Towermax Fitness

    Soundbased virtual reality workout for boxers, martial arts and fitness enthusiasts.

  • CERN CMS / LHC Tour by CERN

    A 360-degree tour of CERN's Large Hadron Collider (LHC) particle acclerator and its Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS).

  • Hubs by Hubs Foundation

    A virtual collaboration platform that lets you create your own 3D spaces. Invite anyone with a URL — no account needed.

  • Stand at the Edge of Geologic Time by NPR

    Transport yourself to Rocky Mountain National Park, with all its sights and sounds, in this immersive geology lesson.

  • A-Painter by A-Frame Team

    Paint in VR with your hands.

  • Above Par-adowski Mini Golf by Paradowski Creative

    A WebXR mini-golf game made for virtual reality.

  • 3D Street by Kieran Farr

    An open-source web-based 3D platform to create and share highly detailed street scenes.

  • Barrier Mage by Doug Reeder

    Draw mystic symbols to burn, protect, or detect & drive off fantastic creatures! [headset required]

  • SimpleDraw by Diarmid Mackenzie

    Create scale drawings and floor plans in 2D, and then view them in VR.

  • Strata by Jingle Chen

    A web-based, real-time, and collaborative spatial computing tool. Think Figma but for XR.
